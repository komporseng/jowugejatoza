    What is Seth Curry’s Net Worth and Salary?

    Seth Curry, the younger brother of Golden State Warriors star Stephen Curry, is an American professional basketball player with a net worth of $16 million. He has played for various teams since he began his professional career in 2013, including the Brooklyn Nets, Sacramento Kings, Dallas Mavericks, and Philadelphia 76ers. Curry’s father, Dell Curry, is also a former NBA player.

    Seth Curry’s Career Earnings Compared to Brother Steph Curry

    Seth Curry earned around $33 million in total salary between his league debut in 2014 and the 2023 season. His 2018-2019 salary with the Portland Trail Blazers was a shade under $3 million, while he earned $8.2 million from the Brooklyn Nets for the 2021-2022 season. In comparison, during those same years, Seth’s brother Steph Curry earned $250 million.

    Seth Curry’s Early Life

    Seth Curry was born in Charlotte, North Carolina on August 23, 1990. His parents are Dell, a former player in the NBA, and Sonya, a former volleyball player. He has an older brother, Stephen, who also played in the NBA, and a younger sister named Sydel who plays volleyball. Curry attended Charlotte Christian School, where he played basketball for three years and averaged 22.3 points per game in his senior year, earning all-conference and all-state honors.

    College Years

    Seth Curry chose to play basketball at Liberty University for his freshman year, where he led all freshmen nationally in average points per game. He then transferred to Duke University, where he spent the remainder of his college years. Curry concluded his senior year being named to the All-ACC first team.

    Seth Curry’s Journey to the NBA

    In the 2013 NBA draft, Curry went undrafted and signed a non-guaranteed contract with the Golden State Warriors, who waived him a few months later. He played for the Santa Cruz Warriors of the NBA D-League and signed with the Memphis Grizzlies, but was waived the same day he made his debut in early 2014. Curry then signed a 10-day contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers before returning to Santa Cruz.

    Curry played for the Orlando Magic in the Orlando Summer League in 2014 and signed with the Magic. He played for the affiliated D-League team the Erie BayHawks, and in 2015, signed a 10-day contract with the Phoenix Suns. He returned to Erie where he averaged 23.8 points per game in his 43 games with the team. Curry signed with the Sacramento Kings for the 2015-16 season and scored a career-high 21 points and notched his first career double-double.

    Seth Curry’s NBA Career Highlights

    Seth Curry signed with the Dallas Mavericks in 2016, recording a new career high of 23 points in a win over the Los Angeles Lakers. He topped that number multiple times, including a high of 31 points in 2017. Curry suffered an injury and was ruled out from the Mavericks in 2017, but returned to the NBA in the 2018-19 season with the Portland Trail Blazers. He helped the team reach the Western Conference Finals but fell to the Golden State Warriors. In 2019, Curry returned to the Dallas Mavericks and posted a career high of 37 points in a game against the Miami Heat. He was then traded to the Philadelphia 76ers and made it to his second NBA playoffs, but the team was eliminated by the Atlanta Hawks. Curry was traded to the Brooklyn Nets in 2022 and made his debut in February with a victory over his former team, the Sacramento Kings.

    Curry’s early success with United States national basketball team

    Before his NBA career, Seth Curry played for the United States national basketball team in the 2009 FIBA Under-19 World Championship held in New Zealand. The team emerged victorious, beating Greece in the final round.

    Curry Ties Knot With Former Volleyball Pro Callie Rivers

    In 2019, Seth Curry, the former Duke Blue Devil and current NBA player, married Callie Rivers, a former professional volleyball player. Rivers is the daughter of Doc Rivers, Curry’s former head coach, and the sister of Austin Rivers, Curry’s former teammate at Duke.

    The couple has two children together, a daughter and a son.
