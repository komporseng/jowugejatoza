    Tulsa Shooting Victim: Who Is Amanda Green? Photos

    Amanda Green was a receptionist at Saint Francis Health System in Tulsa.

    Moreover, she is one of the victims of the mass shooting at the medical center. As per the reports, she was shot because she “came in the way.” The gunman’s primary target was the physician, Dr. Preston Phillips.

    The Tulsa Police have already identified the victims. The other two are Dr. Stephanie Husen, and a patient, William Love. According to several reports, the patient held the door closed assisting in the escape of another injured patient.
