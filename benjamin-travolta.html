    Celebrity Parents: John And Kelly

    The 10-years-old Benjamin, aka Ben, was born silver spoon in his mouth. The reason behind Ben's prominence is because of his renowned parents.

    Ben's father John is the successful Primetime Emmy Award-winning actor. The 67 aged Travolta has worked in films like Magnificent Desolation Grease, etc.

    On the other hand, Benjamin's mother Kelly Preston was also an actress. She had made her appearance in movies and TV shows like Gotti, The Experts, etc.

    Parent's (Kelly And John)'s Marital Details

    Ben's parents Kelly and John met for the first time while filming the 1989 comedy film The Experts. After the first encounter, they shared a good bond and soon start their romantic affair.

    From the wedding day, the husband-wife duo was sharing a healthy bond. Everything was going fine, but Kelly's medical condition, which led her towards death, shattered their marital relationship.

    Shared Three Kids

    Benjamin's elder brother Jett is no more in this world. He died on January 2, 2009, at the age of 16 after suffering a seizure.

    Similarly, John and Kelly became parents for the second time in 2000, who shared a daughter named Ella Bleu Travolta. Their third kid is Benjamin.
