    The second part of Dune began shooting this week while confirming the new additions to the epic cast.

    “With just a little more than a year to go before its theatrical release, the sequel to Denis Villeneuve‘s 2021 hit Dune has finally begun production. The movie announced the start of shooting via its official Twitter account, posting a photo of Dune: Part 2‘s clapper board along with the caption ‘We’re rolling.’”

    The Gray Man star Regé-Jean-Page is open to Netflix recasting his role in Bridgerton.

    “When it was announced that actor Regé-Jean Page would not be returning to Bridgerton, it created a lot of unrest in the fandom that watched the series just to look at him. Still, in the time since, Page has not seemed to regret this choice and is even fine with the character being recast.”

    Read more at The Mary Sue

    Emilia Clarke, star of the upcoming MCU series Secret Invasion, opens up on her experiences with brain aneurysms and counts herself lucky she’s even able to work.

    “Forget her successful post-Game Of Thrones acting career: Emilia Clarke is just grateful to be able to use her voice after suffering two life-threatening brain aneurysms while filming the show. In an interview with BBC’s Sunday Morning, Clarke spoke candidly about her health troubles, and the difficult process of regaining her cognition with ‘quite a bit’ of her brain missing. The actor said, what with the emergency surgeries she’s undergone, it is ‘remarkable’ she still has the ability to speak.”
