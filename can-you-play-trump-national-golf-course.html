    Membership and Access

    One of the key factors that determine your ability to play at Trump National Golf Course is membership. Trump National courses are primarily private clubs, which means that membership is required to access the facilities. These memberships often come with a hefty price tag, making them exclusive to a select few. However, some Trump National courses do offer limited public access on certain days or through special events. It is essential to check with the specific course you are interested in to inquire about their membership policies and public access opportunities.

    Public Tee Times

    While Trump National Golf Course is primarily a private club, some courses within the Trump portfolio do offer public tee times. These tee times are available for non-members to book and provide an opportunity for golf enthusiasts to experience the renowned Trump golfing experience.
