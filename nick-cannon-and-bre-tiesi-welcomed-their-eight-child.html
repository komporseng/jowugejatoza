    On Monday, July 25, the model announced on Instagram that she had given birth to her baby son through “natural unmedicated home delivery.” Cannon, a 41-year-old television star, already has seven other children from four separate relationships. It’s fair to assume he spends a lot of money on child support. While the precise value cannot be ascertained, it is believed to be about $2.2 million every year.

    Cannon revealed his pregnancy with Tiesi on an episode of The Nick Cannon Show, saying:

    “I’m going to be the greatest father I can be.”

    Legendary is the moniker given to the kid who was born on June 28. Bre Tiesi described her experience in an Instagram post:

    “This was the most humbling, limit-pushing, enlightening, and utterly uplifting experience I’ve ever had.”

    The 31-year-old also praised her staff for assisting her in having a safe delivery and announced that her YouTube fans could see her birthing video.

    Nick Cannon’s child support payments are being investigated.

    Goldie Schon, the Wild ‘N Out host’s family law attorney, and legal specialist revealed information concerning Cannon’s child support to sources. According to the lawyer, Cannon has a one-of-a-kind agreement due to his popularity and large revenues. Given Cannon’s yearly salary, she indicated that the California court had the “authority to diverge from the standard child support rules.”

    According to Schon, the court calculates child support using DissoMaster. This guarantees that the kid not only retains the lifestyle provided by the mother but also enjoys the opulent lifestyle provided by the father. The attorney also said that Nick Cannon’s estimated $20 million net wealth is not included when calculating child support. His yearly salary, on the other hand, does not. According to the lawyer:

    “If he earns between $3 and $5 million per year, you have to consider what it would cost to keep the children in the lifestyle of both parents.”

    In terms of Cannon’s baby mom Abbey De La Rose, the lawyer estimates that the mother of twins Zilly and Zion already receives $600,000 to $750,000 in child support, in addition to Cannon’s funding expenditures such as school and child care. Schon estimated that with a third kid on the way, the number would rise:

    “When you factor in her forthcoming third kid, she’ll be earning a cool million a year.”

    Schon also said that having another kid with Rose would be less expensive than having another child with a new lady. Cannon and Brittany Bell have two children. According to the lawyer, he pays her around $60,000 in child support each month. The family attorney also said that Canon is unlikely to pay Mariah Carey child support since she makes more than the television presenter.
