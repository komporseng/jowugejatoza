The family was reportedly joined by several of Lamar’s closest friends, who were invited by Khloe to spend a couple of hours with Lamar on his special day, TMZ reports. “Everyone’s in great spirits, mainly Lamar,” a source told HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “The overwhelming love and support Khloe and her family have shown him is incredibly phenomenal.
