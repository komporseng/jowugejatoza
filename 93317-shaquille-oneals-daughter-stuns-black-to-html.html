    17 May 2024, 00:00

    Mimi O’Neal, one of the daughters of NBA legend Shaquille O’Neal, took to Instagram to share a couple of photos wearing a black ensemble that made her look beautiful.

    The first image of the three-picture slideshow portrayed Mimi in front of a big mirror wearing a black crop-top and matching ripped pants. Apart from that, she chose a beige Gucci fanny pack.

    For the second and the third photo, Mimi was posing in the same way in front of the same mirror. The only aspect that changed was her hair, which was not over her shoulder in the second one.

    In the caption, Mimi wrote a broken heart emoji. As soon as she posted those pictures, her friends and followers took to the comment section to share their thoughts.

    One of them was Instagram user @candybeballin, who pointed out that Mimi looked gorgeous. @eleeyah.t chimed in and said that the sixteen-year-old girl was very pretty.

    Those pictures were uploaded a couple of weeks after Mimi shared a beautiful throwback photo from her parents’ wedding day as a way to celebrate Father’s Day.

    In that image, a much younger Shaq and his then-wife Shaunie were seen holding each other’s hands. Shaunie was wearing a beautiful white dress while Shaq put on a dark suit with a white tie.

    For the caption, Mimi wished a happy Father’s Day to Shaw and Shaunie and added that she appreciated and loved both of them. Mimi finished by thanking her parents for everything they have done for her.

    As Celebritist previously reported, the couple was married from 2002 to 2009, although they initially parted ways in 2007 after the former NBA player filed for divorce. Later, they rekindle their relationship, but Shaunie filed for divorce again in 2009.

    Even though they have their differences and unsolved issues, Shaq and Shaunie know that they must have a good relationship between then to raise their children in the best possible way.

    Fortunately, every time they get together to support their kids, their estranged relationship is put aside.
