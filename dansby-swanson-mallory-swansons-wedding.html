    Dansby Grew Up Supporting The Braves

    Dansby Swanson was born James Dansby Swanson on February 11, 1994, in Kennesaw, Georgia. The American athlete came from an athletic household, as his mom played basketball and tennis at Troy University, and his dad played baseball and worked as an assistant coach for The Trojans. Swanson, who grew up outside of Atlanta, supported Atlanta Braves during childhood and frequently attended matches at Turner Field.

    Swanson represented the baseball and basketball teams of Marietta High School in Georgia as a two-sport varsity player. Recently, he and the Chicago Cubs just reached an agreement on a seven-year, $177 million contract. It was a week before his marriage to NWSL superstar Mallory Pugh.

    Mallory Was Raised In A Supportive Household

    Mallory Swanson was born Mallory Diane Pugh on April 29, 1998, in Colorado, United States. Her elder sister Brianna Pugh reared her in Highland Ranch, Colorado. Mallory comes from a sporting family, just like her hubby, Dansby. Her dad played track and field and soccer, and her mom was a long-distance runner.

    Pugh credits her elder sister for inspiring her to pursue soccer while growing up. She began playing soccer at age four and subsequently joined prestigious clubs, following in her sister’s footsteps. Swanson went to Highlands Ranch’s Mountain Vista High School.
