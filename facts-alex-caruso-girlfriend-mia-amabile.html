    After four seasons with the team, the NBA player Alex Caruso is leaving Los Angeles Lakers, as he is joining Chicago Bulls in a $37 million contract. In the meantime, one of the handsome basketball players, Caruso, also reportedly seems to be busy with his new love, Mia Amabile.

    So, who is Alex Caruso’s girlfriend, Mia Amabile? Here, let’s take a closer look at her life and her relationship with Caruso.

    A Notable Instagram Model

    Alex Caruso’s girlfriend, Mia Amabile, is a stunning Instagram model. As of August 2021, her Instagram page possesses over 264K followers. In addition, each of her glamorous photos on the site gets over 5K likes within a few hours after she posts it.

    So, many prestigious fashion and beauty care companies choose Mia Amabile to do modeling for them and advertise their products via her Instagram page. Some of the companies whose products Mia advertises include Missguided USA, Paige, Briogeo Hair Care, Dossier Perfumes, Pretty Little Thing, and many more.

    Besides being popular on Instagram, Mia Amabile is also a TikTok star, with 175.6K followers and over 3.3 million likes at the moment.

    Alex Caruso’s Girlfriend Is A Social Media Coordinator

    According to the Linkedin page of Alex Caruso’s partner, Mia Amabile is currently a social media coordinator at LF stores. She joined in 2018 and her responsibilities at LF Stores include posting all social media content for both Carmar Denim and Emma & Sam. Also, she schedules, styles, and shoots all social media photoshoots with models.

    Before joining the LA-based LF Stores, Mia Amabile was a wholesale manager at Ete Swimwear and Siempre Golden from 2017 to 2018. Moreover, Amabile also served in many other companies in different posts to earn a living.

    Previously, she served as an intern at Project Social T, Cornerstone Professional Liability Consultants, and Free People. Also, Amabile worked as a waitress at Avalon Coffee Company, Inc. for some years in the past.

    Moreover, Alex Caruso’s girlfriend, Mia Amabile, earned an Economics degree from the San Diego State University in 2018. According to Amabile’s Facebook page, she is also a graduate of Griffith University and The Shipley School.

    Made It Official In July 2021

    As noted by various online sources, Alex Caruso and his girlfriend, Mia Amabile, made their relationship official in August 2021. Both Alex and Mia had never shared any pictures with one another on their social networking handles before.

    However, at the end of July 2021, Amabile uploaded a photo of her getting cozy with the former Lakers guard on her Instagram story, which pretty much confirmed their romance.

    Besides, going through the Instagram posts of Mia Amabile, we can see Alex Caruso offering flirtatious comments on her latest Instagram posts. It hints that the romantic duo might have been dating each other for a long time before they decided to make it public.

    However, neither Alex Caruso nor his girl, Mia Amabile, has disclosed more about their bonding. It seems like the public needs to wait some more time to know about this pair’s romantic life.

    Alex Caruso’s Previous Relationships

    Before Alex Caruso was linked with model Mia Amabile, he was reportedly in an intimate relationship with Abby Brewer, born Abigail Katherine Brewer. Currently, she serves as a Senior Account Executive at The Power Group, a small public relations, and communications company.

    Going through the social media posts by Abby, it looks like it had been more than a year since she started hanging out with Alex Caruso. In 2019, she had posted a couple of photos with Caruso mentioning him as her man. However, lately, Abby has not shared any snaps with him or mentioned him in her posts.

    So, a sudden appearance of Alex Caruso on Mia Amabile’s Instagram, and his absence from Abby Brewer’s social media posts, indicates that he is in a relationship with Amabile at the moment.

    Hopefully, Alex will open up more about his new girl and his budding relationship with her shortly.
